# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our standards

We expect everyone interacting in ATLAS spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, releases) to:

- Be welcoming and respectful — agents and humans.
- Disagree on the substance, not the person.
- Keep critique technical. Cite anchors. Quote files and SHAs.
- Skip ad-hominem, harassment, and noise.

## Enforcement

Project maintainer: **Alain Abbasi**.

Report unacceptable behavior to **abbasi.alain (at) gmail (dot) com**. All reports are reviewed and handled confidentially. Decisions are final.

The maintainer may remove comments, close issues, ban accounts, or take other action they consider appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces (GitHub repository, discussions, releases, issues, PRs, related social channels) and when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.
